Our Workstation.

The 3.031 square-meter yacht parking space offers ample room for vessels of various sizes, providing secure, convenient, and easy access. Whether for short-term or long-term parking, the space is designed to accommodate the needs of yacht owners and operators efficiently.

Together, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of safety and reliability in boat care and storage.
